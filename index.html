  • Cipher reached for the belt — fell 20 ft. into a covered pit trap with goo-coated punji stakes (12 piercing; Feather Fall saved fall damage)
  • Kragar's Divine Sense: multiple fiends detected
  • Six gnolls rose from camouflage on three sides, then three demonic gnolls burst from the jungle (glowing eyes, skull-flail weapons)
  • Bart's Hypnotic Pattern accidentally charmed Cipher (DC 13 Wis — failed)
  • Salida dropped one gnoll immediately with poisoned arrows
  • Bart took repeated bites and poison — went unconscious
  • Sylvar and Ashclaw both poofed (summons lost)
  • Kragar: Branding Smite + Divine Smite combo (~50 damage on one gnoll); deployed Spiritual Weapon
  • Ahdran: burned two Lucky dice for a crit that killed a demonic gnoll in a burst of hellfire
  • One gnoll escaped to the southeast

Post-combat: Kragar stabilized and healed Bart. Note: Lay on Hands doesn't work on Cipher (construct).

Items to identify: Bloodstained leather belt (possibly magical) · Green object, possibly a sending stone (still in the pit)

Open threads: Who is the mysterious traveler worshipping Meliki? What are the canine howls from the southeast? Will the escaped gnoll return with reinforcements?

World Lore & Factions

🌙 The Death Curse

A plague spreading across the world. Anyone who was previously raised from the dead is now actually dying again. The magic to bring people back from death no longer works. Caused by the Soul Monger artifact somewhere in Chult, which harvests the souls of the dead for an unknown entity. Syndra (who was previously raised) is dying because of it. The entire land/continent of Chult is dying as its soul-energy is consumed.

🐍 Dendar the Night Serpent

An ancient Primordial entity — the opposite of Ubtao (god of Chult). Imprisoned since before recorded history. It is prophesied that Dendar will one day break free and swallow the sun, ending this plane of existence. During a recent eclipse, her eye appeared in the sky — a reptilian pupil looking around. Ras-Nsi and the Yuan-ti are trying to free her. Her Spawn is imprisoned in the Plane of Earth.

🏛️ Ubtao

Creator god of Chult. The dinos are called "Children of Ubtao." Made the maze system (maze balls connect to Ubtao's understanding). The Aldani people were cursed by Ubtao and turned into crab-creatures. The Heart of Ubtao is a floating rock structure near Mbala. There are snake waypoint pillars throughout Chult — touching the fang gives a tattoo/waypoint. The Naga Saja N'Baza served Ubtao.

🐍 The Yuan-Ti (Wantee)

Snake-people who serve Ras-Nsi. Can create magical portals connecting to the Plane of Earth. Led by Ras-Nsi, an undead yuan-ti who was once a Paladin of Ubtao. Attacked Port Nyanzaru. The ancient carved stones with snake symbols are their markers. Their portals allowed them to find Dendar's Spawn's prison.

🧙 Red Wizards of Thay

Have a base of operations at the Heart of Ubtao. Mekel is their representative in Chult. Were involved in capturing Axel (who was teleported from the Heart of Ubtao). Interested in the Shambling Mounds. Ahdran hates them (Harper allegiance). Red Wizards had explored and mapped Chult previously — "so many wizards in Chult."

🔑 The Maze / Ubtao's Understanding

A recurring motif. The maze ball Cipher carries connects to something deeper. Cipher heard "Curious" twice during meditation — an entity watching him. The maze ball allows: 1x/day ritual traverse (10 min), found Expeditious Retreat inside it, has a vault door where energy is stored. The ring from the dino race had a maze on it. The stone labyrinth in the jungle led to a tomb. There's "more to find" per the blessing received.

🗺️ The City of Omu

Ancient capital city of Chult. Lost in the jungle, sunken below the canopy. Can only be spotted from high ground or the air. Located far to the south. Contains the Soul Monger device. Nanny Pu'pu knows someone who might know where it is. The Sewn Sisters are "all over that area." Known also as the "Peaks of Flame and Valley of Lost Honor."

🦅 Factions in Chult

  • The Harpers — Ahdran's faction. Syndra's ally is a Harper. Someone in the Harpers wants Ahdran dead or captured.
  • Order of the Gauntlet — Undril Silvertusk's faction. Worship Torm and Tyr. Camps: Vengeance and Righteous.
  • Flaming Fist — Run Fort Beluarian. Commander Lyara was in league with pirates.
  • Merchant Princes — 7 merchant princes control Port Nyanzaru (Wakanga, Jobal, Kwayothe, Ekene-Afa, Efon Talroa, Jesamine, Johnthe).
  • Pirates — Based at Jahaka Anchorage. Sell slaves. Were in league with Flaming Fist command.
  • Batiri Goblins — Various tribes. Biting Ant Tribe had the Shield Guardian amulet. Queen Grabstab educated on Sword Coast.

🗝️ Snake Waypoint System

Snake pillars (Yaunti/Ubtao origin) are scattered throughout Chult. Touch the fang → receive a magical tattoo with the location's unique runes. Line up symbols on the pillar for the destination → portal opens. Kragar has multiple waypoints tattooed. The Yaunti also use these portals (and have additional "corridor" branches). One portal goes near the port; another is in the area north of the goblin camp.
